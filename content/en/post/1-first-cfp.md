We are pleased to announce that the _Knowledge Graphs and Large Language Models_ workshop has been accepted as a full day workshop during [LREC 2026 conference](https://lrec2026.info).

LREC 2026 conference workshops will take place during the pre-conference and post-conference days, **11-12 May** and **16 May** respectively. We do not know yet the exact date for KG & LLM @ LREC 2026 but will update the website as soon as we know.

This website will further evolve to include Call for Papers, Author kits and program of the workshop. In the mean time, stay tuned for practical way to submit your papers.

Looking further to see you all in Palma de Mallorca.

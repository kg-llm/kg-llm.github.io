With the rather high submission number, we worked hard and decided to Desk Reject 2 submissions that were out of the 
workshop scope or were exhibiting a high number of fake citations.

Our [Program Chair members](/pc) did a tremendous job reading, evaluating and providing feedback to authors in a very short time.

All this work allowed us to select 21 submissions as Oral or Poster presentations, leading to a 61% acceptance rate.

The submissions span a wide range of topics, showing the very high diversity of approaches mixing Knowledge Graphs 
and Large Language Models in current research trends. With such a selection, we know we will have an 
interesting time in Palma.
